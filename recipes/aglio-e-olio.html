Aglio e Olio

Description

This is a basic italian recipe that requires few ingredients and fewer steps to make.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. While waiting for water to boil, slice garlic in thin sheets.
  3. Separate parsley leaves from the stalks. Finely chop the leaves without crushing while cutting the stalks into very short pieces.
  4. When the water is boiling or close to boiling, add salt; a handful or two depending on the size of pot.
  5. Allow the water to boil for 1 minute before adding the Spaghetti. Boil the sphagetti for 1 minute less than what is recommended on the package.
  6. In a pan, heat the olive oil on lowest setting for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add sliced garlic and parsley stalks.
  8. Turn off the fire when the garlic starts to brown. Wait for the Sphagetti to cook.
  9. Transfer the sphagetti from the pot directly to the pan. Mix it well with the oil.
  10. Add two and a half cup of pasta water from the pot into the pan.
  11. Turn on the fire and set it to medium.
  12. Add the parsley leaves and red pepper flakes while continually mixing the spaghetti and oil.
  13. Once the pasta water and oil mixture thickens, turn off the fire. Continue mixing until all water is absorbed and the sauce is mostly oil.
  14. Transfer to a bowl or tray.
