Fried Fish

Description

Simple but very tasty way of cooking fish.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Rub salt on fish.
  2. On pan, heat a half cup of oil.
  3. Put fish on pan.
  4. Wait for fish to turn golden brown then turn it over.
  5. Wait for the other side to turn golden brown, then remove from pan and transfer to plate.
